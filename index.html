E-SUMMIT is the flagship event of Entrepreneurship Development Cell. Entrepreneurship Summit is the brainchild of E-Cell, BV(DU)COEP. E-Summit is celebrated as the festival of entrepreneurship all accross Pune.

+ It was first started in 2016, and has been rising since then. Aiming at inculcation and development, the very spirit of entrepreneurship, E-Cell BV(DU)COEP comes up with its annual affair to stir an essence of teamwork and synergy in the masses. We believe that for the students to develop in an all round manner, it becomes absolutely imperative to connect them with the competitive corporate world.

It invites young entrepreneurs, budding talents, corporal people to exchange ideas and views regarding entrepreneurship. Through E-Summit we have been able to connect the students with the corporate ecosystem. It has inspired young generation with a new zeal towards entrepreneurship.
